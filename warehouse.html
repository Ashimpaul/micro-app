Micro Warehousing

Micro warehousing offers small-scale storage solutions ranging from as little as 1 sqm (20 kg) to 100 sqm (40 MT) or more, strategically positioned near customer locations. This approach ensures faster delivery, reduced shipping costs, and enhanced customer satisfaction.

By storing inventory closer to end customers, micro warehousing accelerates order fulfillment, meeting the demands of a rapidly evolving market driven by e-commerce, quick commerce (q-commerce), and a highly mobile society. It addresses challenges like limited warehouse space, rising delivery expectations, urbanization, and frequent replenishment needs.

These facilities are also ideal for local businesses and residents as convenient hubs for pick-up, drop-off, and short-term storage. Micro warehousing is a practical, scalable solution tailored to the needs of today's fast-paced world.
